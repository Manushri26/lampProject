    About

    Synergy provides facilities tailored to the age group it serves. Without the distraction of much younger or much older students nearby, students can focus on building meaningful peer relationships. Located in Mumbai, we are an independent, coeducational, college-preparatory academy with divisions through grade 12. Our mission is to develop leaders of character through superior academics and spiritual growth.Highly-trained faculty, state-of-the-art resources and innovative curriculum have resulted in distinctive programs that make a special attraction.

    Arts


    Students have many opportunities to pursue their interests beyond the curriculum. The School offers arts activities—photography, sculpture, ceramics, dramatic productions, orchestra and chorus, for example—in addition to the regular courses.

    Academics


    Students may participate in math, robotics and engineering clubs or join Interschool advanced math courses as juniors or seniors. A student may also pursue an extracurricular project or internship that worthily replaces some or all of their academic work.

    Sports


    Athletic Program is designed to be introductory in nature by placing an emphasis on sport-specific skill development. It provides students with an opportunity to experience a variety of teams and individual sports in an intramural and interscholastic setting.
