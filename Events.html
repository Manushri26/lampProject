    ONLINE EVENTS FROM APRIL 16 TO APRIL 28

                

    ALL ALUMNAE INVITED!

    Check back often for updates and additions to the program, such as faculty-led classes, live student class drop-ins and additional panels.

    All times are Indian Standard Time.

    Please join us for our first virtual Reunion!

    Because COVID-19 impacted last year’s Reunion, we were determined to deliver a full 2021 Reunion program, which would not only honor our classes ending in 1 and 6, but also include last year’s reunion classes ending in 0 and 5.

    We are excited to present a robust program, still in formation, which will be thought provoking and informative and provide opportunities for connection in ways we could never have envisioned in a “typical” year. We planned events over a 13-day period and at various times not only to reduce consecutive hours online, but to also make Reunion 2021 accessible to more alums across India, and worldwide. We hope you will join for as many events as possible.

    Please reach out with any questions.
